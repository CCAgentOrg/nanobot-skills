# Attention-Grabbing Hooks

## Question Hooks

- "Ever wonder why [X]?"
- "Why does everyone [X] when [Y] is better?"
- "What if I told you [X]?"
- "Can we talk about [X] for a second?"
- "Why is [X] so hard?"
- "Am I the only one who [X]?"
- "What's the deal with [X]?"
- "Have you noticed that [X]?"

## Statement Hooks

- "I just shipped something new."
- "Here's an unpopular opinion:"
- "Let's be honest about [X]."
- "Stop doing [X]. Start doing [Y]."
- "The secret to [X] is simple."
- "Most people get [X] wrong."
- "I wasted [time] on [X] so you don't have to."
- "This changed everything for me."

## Data/Number Hooks

- "3 things I learned from [X]"
- "7 reasons why [X] works"
- "I analyzed [N] [things] and found [result]"
- "[Percentage] of [people] are missing this"
- "Spent [hours] hours so you don't have to"
- "Reduced [metric] by [percentage]"
- "[Number] tools to [action]"
- "[X] vs [Y]: Here's the difference"

## Surprise Hooks

- "Nobody talks about this but..."
- "The hidden cost of [X]"
- "What nobody tells you about [X]"
- "The truth about [X] that nobody mentions"
- "Here's what [experts] don't want you to know"
- "The real reason [X] happens"
- "This one trick changed everything"

## Story Hooks

- "I used to [X]. Then I discovered [Y]."
- "Here's how I went from [state A] to [state B]"
- "The story of how [X] happened"
- "Last week I tried [X]. Here's what happened."
- "6 months ago I started [X]. Today [result]."

## Problem/Solution Hooks

- "Struggling with [X]? Here's the fix."
- "Tired of [X]? Try this instead."
- "The [pain point] nobody talks about"
- "Finally fixed [X]. Here's how."
- "Solved [X] in [time]. Here's the approach."

## Curiosity Hooks

- "The most underrated [thing] in [domain]"
- "You're probably [doing something wrong] without knowing"
- "The [thing] that changed my [work/life]"
- "What if [X] was actually [Y]?"
- "Here's something most people miss"

## Emotion Hooks

- "I'm tired of [X]."
- "Can we please stop [X]?"
- "Finally. [Result]."
- "This frustrates me: [X]"
- "I have a confession about [X]"

## Direct Hooks

- "Let me be clear about [X]"
- "Here's the truth about [X]"
- "I'm going to say this once"
- "This needs to be said"

## "Stop" Hooks

- "Stop using [X]. Use [Y] instead."
- "Stop obsessing over [X]. Focus on [Y]."
- "Stop [action]. Start [better action]."
- "Stop reading [X]. Read [Y] instead."

## "Start" Hooks

- "Start with [Y], not [X]."

## Usage Tips

1. **Match the hook to the content** - Don't use a curiosity hook for a straightforward announcement
2. **Keep hooks short** - 1-2 lines max
3. **Make it authentic** - Don't use clickbait that doesn't deliver
4. **Test different types** - Questions work well for engagement, statements for authority
5. **Consider your audience** - Devs respond to different hooks than marketers

## Combining Hooks

You can layer hook types:
- Question + Number: "Why do 90% of devs [X]?"
- Statement + Data: "Most people get [X] wrong. Here's why..."
- Story + Curiosity: "I used to [X]. Then I discovered [Y]."
